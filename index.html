Approach

Technology, learning, and design working together intentionally.

My work sits at the intersection of educational technology, systems thinking, accessibility, AI adoption, and user-centered design. I translate complex tools and emerging technologies into clear learning support ecosystems that educators can use with confidence.

The throughline is practical design: resources should be structured enough to scale, polished enough to communicate trust, and simple enough to support real teaching workflows under real constraints.

Working Approach

  • Design for real workflows, not abstract feature lists.
  • Use AI as a learning design and productivity question, not only a policy issue.
  • Build resources that are accessible, searchable, reusable, and easy to maintain.
  • Balance innovation with governance, clarity, and institutional context.
